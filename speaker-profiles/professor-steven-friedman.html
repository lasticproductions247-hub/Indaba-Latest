Perspective

"South Africa will not deal effectively with poverty and inequality while those who need change most remain unheard." Professor Steven Friedman is a South African political scientist whose work focuses on democracy, inequality, citizenship and public voice.

Background

Professor Steven Friedman is a Research Professor at the University of Johannesburg and a leading voice in South African public discourse on democracy and social justice. His research and writing examine the relationship between democracy, inequality, and citizenship in South Africa and the Global South. He has authored numerous books and academic papers on these topics, contributing significantly to public debate on the country's democratic trajectory.

Organisation

University of Johannesburg — Professor Steven Friedman is a Research Professor at the University of Johannesburg, where his work focuses on democracy, inequality, citizenship and the relationship between public voice and social justice in South Africa.
