Perspective

Background

Fikile Kuhlase is a development finance and transformation specialist with more than thirty years of experience working across socio-economic development. She has built a career around advancing practical, system-aware approaches to change, with a strong emphasis on partnerships, governance and the long-term sustainability of development initiatives. Her experience positions her as a steady voice in conversations about how capital and community needs can be better aligned for lasting impact.

Organisation

Kgolo Community Development Trust — Kgolo Community Development Trust supports community-focused development work rooted in long-term transformation, partnership and practical impact.
