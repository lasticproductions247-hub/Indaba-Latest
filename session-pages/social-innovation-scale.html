Day 2 · Systems Reimagination

Social Innovation at Scale: Designing Solutions for South Africa's Structural Challenges

As South Africa grapples with persistent inequality, traditional approaches to development are no longer enough. This session explores how social innovation can unlock scalable, cross-sector solutions by bringing together business, government, civil society and communities to address the root causes of inequality. Rather than funding isolated projects, the discussion focuses on designing innovative systems that create lasting social and economic impact across South Africa's most pressing structural challenges.
